Singularity

What is singularity?

A black hole singularity is a theoretical point at the center of a black hole where matter is compressed into zero volume and infinite density, causing all laws of physics as we know them to break down. It is not a point in space, but rather a moment in time and the end of time for anything that crosses the event horizon. The existence and exact nature of singularities are not fully understood, and a new theory of quantum gravity is needed to fully describe them.
